Brief Biography

Jun Zeng is a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering Department at the University of California, Berkeley. He has been working on various problem in optimization, control, planning, learning.

He received a BS in Mechanical Engineering and Automation from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2016, and Diplome d'Ingeinur from Ecole Polytechnique in 2017 with X Fondation Fellowship from 2015 to 2017. At Berkeley, He was recipient of Graduate Division Block Fellowship in both 2020 and 2021, and Chin Leung Shui Chun Fellowship in 2021.
