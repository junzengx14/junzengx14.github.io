Biography

I'm a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering Department at the University of California, Berkeley. I have been working on various problems in optimization, control, planning, learning.

I received a BS in Mechanical Engineering and Automation from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2016, and Diplome d'Ingeinur from Ecole Polytechnique in 2017 with X Fondation Fellowship from 2015 to 2017. At Berkeley, I was recipient of Graduate Division Block Fellowship in both 2020 and 2021, and Chin Leung Shui Chun Fellowship in 2021.

I usually host several ongoing research projects, please feel free to reach out to me through emails if you are willing to join the projects.
