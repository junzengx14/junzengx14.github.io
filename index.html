Jun Zeng

zengjunsjtu AT berkeley DOT edu / Google Scholar / GitHub / Twitter
I'm a Ph.D. student in Hybrid Robotics Lab advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath. My research focues on control, motion planning and optimization for hybrid systems, especially for aerial manipulation and legged robotics. I'm also interested in theoretical problems about safety-critical control using control barrier functions.
