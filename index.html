Jun Zeng

zengjunsjtu AT berkeley DOT edu / Google Scholar / GitHub / Twitter
From summer 2022, I will join Cruise as a senior software engineer, where I will contribute to control and planning for autonomous vehicles.

At Berkeley, I've worked in Hybrid Robotics Group advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath. My research lies on control, motion planning and optimization for hybrid nonlinear systems, especially for aerial manipulation and legged robotics.
