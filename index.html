Jun Zeng

zengjunsjtu AT berkeley DOT edu / Google Scholar / GitHub / Twitter
I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in Hybrid Robotics Group advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath. My research lies on control, motion planning and optimization for hybrid systems, especially for aerial manipulation and legged robotics. I'm also interested in theoretical problems about safety-critical control using control barrier functions.
